About

Computer science is the study of computers and computational systems. It encompasses a wide range of topics, including algorithms and data structures, programming languages, software engineering, computer architecture, artificial intelligence, and more. It is a rapidly evolving field that is critical to many areas of modern society, including business, medicine, and entertainment.
